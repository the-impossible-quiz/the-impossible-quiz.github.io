Experience Puzzle Fun with 11-11

Prepare for a challenging puzzle adventure in 11-11, where your goal is to strategically place blocks on the board to earn points. As you drag colorful blocks onto the grid, your task is to group them together to complete rows and columns. Keep a sharp eye on the shapes and make sure you don't run out of space as you aim for high scores in this addictive puzzle game!

Test Your Spatial Skills

With each move, you'll need to carefully consider the placement of the blocks to maximize your points. As the board fills up, the challenge intensifies, putting your spatial awareness and strategic thinking to the test. Can you outsmart the game and achieve the highest score?

How to Play 11-11

Using your mouse cursor, select, drag, and place the blocks onto the board. Plan your moves wisely to create complete rows and columns, earning points as you go. With its intuitive controls and engaging gameplay, 11-11 offers hours of puzzle-solving entertainment for players of all ages.
