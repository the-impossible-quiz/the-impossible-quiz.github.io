Description

Experience Thrilling Races in 3D Car Simulator

3D Car Simulator offers heart-pounding racing action where you can unleash the full potential of modified vehicles across the globe! Take the wheel of various high-performance cars, including rally cars, police vehicles, and muscle cars, each with its own unique characteristics.

Explore Diverse Environments

Embark on adrenaline-fueled adventures across three expansive maps, each offering its own challenges and scenic vistas. Whether you're tearing through city streets or navigating off-road terrain, there's always something new to discover.

Immersive Visuals and Realistic Effects

Immerse yourself in the world of 3D Car Simulator with stunning graphics and lifelike effects that enhance the racing experience. From dynamic lighting to realistic vehicle physics, every detail is designed to keep you on the edge of your seat.

Masterful Vehicle Handling

Feel the thrill of high-speed driving with smooth handling that puts you in complete control of your chosen vehicle. Switch between cars seamlessly to adapt to different terrains and driving conditions, ensuring an exhilarating ride every time.

How to Play

Take command of your car using the WASD or arrow keys for movement. Use the space bar to engage the handbrake, C to change the view, and Shift to shift gears. When you need to reset, simply press R for the game or G for the bike. Show off your police lights with the press of the E key!
