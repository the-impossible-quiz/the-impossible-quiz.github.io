Introduction to A Small World Cup

A Small World Cup offers a delightful twist on traditional soccer games, merging the quirky physics of "Mutilate a Doll" with the excitement of football. Created by Rujo Games, this two-player soccer game challenges you to score goals using rag-doll characters, resulting in hilarious and unpredictable gameplay.

Experience Unconventional Soccer Fun

Step onto the field with your rag-doll team and prepare for a unique soccer experience like no other. Embrace the chaos as you slam your character against the ball in an attempt to score goals and outmaneuver your opponent.

Compete in Various Modes

A Small World Cup offers multiple game modes to keep you entertained. Whether you choose to practice your skills or compete for the world cup, there's something for everyone. Test your abilities in Golden Goal mode, where scoring just one goal can lead you to victory.

Customize Your Experience

With the ability to select difficulty levels, teams, and tournament stages, A Small World Cup allows you to tailor your gaming experience to your preferences. Take on the challenge and see if you have what it takes to emerge victorious.

How to Play

Playing A Small World Cup is straightforward:

  • Pick Player: Left-click to select your rag-doll player.
  • Drag Player: Use your cursor to drag your player across the field.

Get ready for a hilarious and entertaining soccer showdown in A Small World Cup!
