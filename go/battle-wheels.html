Introduction to Battle Wheels

Prepare for intense vehicular combat in Battle Wheels, an adrenaline-pumping arcade game where you engage in one-on-one battles against your foes! Take control of your car and unleash mayhem as you aim to smash your opponent's vehicle. With no roofs to hinder your attacks, use your vehicle's maneuverability to outwit and outmaneuver your adversaries.

Upgrade and Customize Your Ride

In Battle Wheels, strategic upgrades can turn the tide of battle in your favor. Enhance your chassis for increased durability, equip powerful wheels for devastating damage, and fine-tune your car with various upgrades to dominate the arena. Customize your character and craft the ultimate battle machine tailored to your playstyle.

Dominate the Arena

Test your skills in action-packed battles and ascend the ranks to become the ultimate Battle Wheels champion. Whether you're competing solo against AI opponents or challenging a friend in two-player mode, every victory brings you closer to glory.

How to Play

In Battle Wheels, victory is achieved by dealing damage to your opponent's vehicle until their health bar reaches zero. Crush your foe's car with precise strikes and emerge victorious in each round!

Controls

Get started in Battle Wheels with these controls:

  • Player 1: Use the A and D keys for action.
  • Player 2: Utilize the Left and Right arrow keys for action.

Step into the arena, unleash destruction, and claim victory in Battle Wheels!
