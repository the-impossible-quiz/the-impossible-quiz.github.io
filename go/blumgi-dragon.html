Description

Welcome to Blumgi Dragon!

Embark on an exhilarating adventure in Blumgi Dragon, a charming action game where you take on the role of various adorable dragons facing off against bomb enemies in thrilling arena battles! Your mission? Rescue fellow dragons captured by these explosive foes and emerge victorious as the ultimate dragon master!

Engage in Arena Battles

Prepare for heart-pounding action as you engage in fast-paced arena battles against bomb enemies. With a simple tap or click, unleash fireballs to fend off adversaries and teleport to strategic positions to outmaneuver your foes.

Team Up with Friends

Join forces with friends and control your dragons together to overcome challenging obstacles and emerge triumphant in your quest to rescue captured dragons. Cooperation and teamwork are key to success in Blumgi Dragon!

How to Play Blumgi Dragon

Take control of your dragon with ease by simply clicking your mouse or tapping the space bar to fire fireballs and teleport. Master the controls and unleash your dragon's full potential!
