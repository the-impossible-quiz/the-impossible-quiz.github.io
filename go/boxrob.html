Description

Join the Cargo Loading Adventure in BoxRob

BoxRob, crafted by 7Spot Games, invites you to a captivating puzzle platform experience right in your browser. Your mission? Load cargo onto a truck using your versatile forklift. Each level presents a challenge: collect all the boxes and deposit them into their designated slots. While some levels may start easy, the difficulty ramps up as you progress, testing your skills and strategic thinking. Maneuver through the intricacies of each level, performing special moves and following sequences to solve puzzles and advance. Share the excitement of BoxRob with your friends and compete to see who can achieve the highest scores!

How to Play BoxRob

Get into the action with simple controls:

  • Move left/right - A/D
  • Jump - W
  • Pick up boxes - Tap or left mouse button click
