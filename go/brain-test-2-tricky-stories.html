Description

Embark on Tricky Adventures with Brain Test 2: Tricky Stories

Join the colorful characters and dive into a world of brain-teasing puzzles in Brain Test 2: Tricky Stories, a captivating puzzle game crafted by Unico Studio. Prepare to exercise your cognitive skills with a myriad of tricky questions and challenges that will put your problem-solving abilities to the test!

A World of Intriguing Stories

Each puzzle in Brain Test 2: Tricky Stories comes with its own unique story, featuring vibrant characters like Tom the Cat, Emily the Farmer, and Joe the Monster Hunter. From facing off against the Lion King to cultivating farms and hunting monsters, embark on thrilling adventures that will challenge your wit and creativity.

Think Outside the Box

To conquer the puzzles, you'll need to think on your feet and approach each problem from a different angle. Utilize everything you see on the screen as a potential clue or solution, and don't be afraid to think outside the box!

How to Play Brain Test 2: Tricky Stories

Engage with the game by using your mouse cursor or finger to select, drag, and manipulate objects on the screen. Uncover hidden clues, solve complex riddles, and showcase your genius to friends as you navigate through the captivating world of Brain Test 2: Tricky Stories!
