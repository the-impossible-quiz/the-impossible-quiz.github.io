Welcome to Burger Bounty!

Step into the shoes of a budding entrepreneur and embark on an exciting culinary journey with Burger Bounty, a captivating simulation game that puts you in charge of your very own burger joint!

Build Your Burger Empire

Start from humble beginnings and gradually expand your restaurant empire by adding more tables, menu items, and team members. Keep a watchful eye on your customers' needs and ensure prompt service to keep them satisfied and your profits rolling in.

Customize and Upgrade

Personalize your character and restaurant to make it truly your own. Unlock and upgrade stations, hire waiters, and invest in hoverboard transportation to glide effortlessly around your establishment, ensuring smooth operations and customer satisfaction.

Experience the Ultimate Culinary Adventure

Burger Bounty combines the thrill of time management gameplay with the strategic depth of business simulation, offering a unique and immersive gaming experience for foodies and aspiring entrepreneurs alike. Can you rise to the challenge and conquer the city's culinary scene?

How to Play Burger Bounty

On desktop, navigate using the arrow keys or W, A, S, D keys. On mobile, simply drag and move to play the game. Get ready to take your burger empire to new heights!
