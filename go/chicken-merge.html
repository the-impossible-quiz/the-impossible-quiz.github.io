Introducing Chicken Merge

Chicken Merge combines merge game mechanics with tower defense elements, challenging you to defend your base against waves of enemies using an army of chickens. Deploy and strengthen your chicken units to fend off the relentless hordes and emerge victorious.

Merge and Defend

Train and improve your chicken units by merging identical chickens into stronger ones. Strategically position your upgraded chickens along the defense lines to create an impenetrable barrier against enemy attacks. With each merge, your army grows stronger, enhancing your chances of survival.

Unleash the Power of Chickens

As you progress, unlock better upgrades and weapons to bolster your arsenal and fortify your defenses. Experiment with different chicken combinations and defense strategies to find the most effective way to repel the invaders.

Challenge Your Friends

Share Chicken Merge with your friends and embark on a journey to see who can build the strongest coop and fend off the most formidable foes. Test your skills and creativity in this addictive blend of merging and defense.

How to Play Chicken Merge

Drag and drop identical chickens to merge them into stronger units. Strategically position your upgraded chickens along the defense lines to protect your base from incoming enemies. Continuously merge and reinforce your chicken army until they become unstoppable.
