Experience Farming Fun in Cow Bay

Step into the hooves of a farmer cow in Cow Bay, an engaging idle simulation game set in a vibrant countryside. Your mission? Transform an empty but fertile plot of land into a thriving settlement under the guidance of the governorâ€”a wise cat who oversees the region. Embark on a journey from humble beginnings, harvesting berries and gathering logs, to tackling more complex tasks like planting seeds and crafting essential tools.

Expand and Explore

As you complete quests assigned by the governor, you'll earn coins to unlock and explore additional islands teeming with opportunities. Discover new features such as crafting tables for creating tools, campfires to cook energy-replenishing recipes, and storage boxes to manage surplus items. Each task you undertake consumes energy, so strategize wisely to maximize your productivity while maintaining your resources.

How to Play Cow Bay

Engage with the game by tapping or clicking on various objects to harvest resources or utilize them for crafting. Whether it's collecting berries from shrubs, gathering logs, or interacting with buildings and workbenches, every action contributes to the growth of your burgeoning settlement. Dive into this tranquil farming adventure on Poki and share the experience with friends to see who can cultivate the most prosperous settlement!
