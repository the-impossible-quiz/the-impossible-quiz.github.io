Description

Welcome to the Dinosaur Game!

Step into the prehistoric world of the Dino Game, an exciting endless runner originally hidden within Google Chrome. Created as an Easter egg in 2014 to entertain users during internet downtime, this game - affectionately known as Chrome Dino - has captured the hearts of over 270 million players worldwide each month.

Embark on an Epic Adventure

Take control of a fearless t-rex as you dash through a desolate landscape filled with obstacles and challenges. Test your reflexes and agility as you guide your dinosaur through an endless journey, overcoming hurdles and dodging hazards along the way.

Play Anytime, Anywhere

Now available on Poki, you can enjoy the Dinosaur Game in fullscreen mode, whether you're online or offline. Play on your PC, mobile phone, or tablet, and experience the thrill of the chase wherever you go!

How to Play

To begin your adventure, simply press the spacebar on your keyboard or tap the dino on your mobile device. Use the spacebar to jump over obstacles and avoid hazards as you strive to achieve the highest score possible. Are you ready to unleash your inner dino and conquer the challenges that await?
