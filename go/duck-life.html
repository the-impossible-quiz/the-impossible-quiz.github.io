Welcome to Duck Life!

Embark on an exciting journey to save the future of the Duck Life 1 farm. Your mission: train your duck to become a champion in running, flying, and swimming. Only by honing its skills can you rescue the farm from jeopardy.

Train Your Duck

Level up your duck by navigating through various training courses. Enhance its abilities in running, swimming, flying, and energy. Click to jump over obstacles during running training, drag your duck up and down while flying, and use the arrow keys to maneuver through water obstacles during swimming sessions.

Boost Your Duck's Energy

Keep your duck energized by feeding it skill seed or normal seed. A higher energy level ensures your duck's endurance during races, allowing it to compete at its best.

Race to Victory

Once your duck has reached proficiency in each skill category, it's time to put its training to the test in thrilling races. Take on competitors and strive for victory to secure the future of the farm. Are you ready to lead your duck to glory?
