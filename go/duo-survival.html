Welcome to Duo Survival!

Duo Survival is an adrenaline-pumping cooperative game developed by 7Spot Games, where you and a friend must guide two survivors through a zombie-infested world teeming with obstacles. As apocalypse survivors, you'll face a relentless horde of hungry zombies, navigating through challenging levels rife with puzzles and dangers.

Embark on a Thrilling Adventure

Team up with a friend to tackle the challenges of this post-apocalyptic landscape. Work together to solve puzzles, activate mechanisms, and overcome obstacles as you strive to lead the survivors to safety and the elusive cure for the virus.

Determine the Fate of Humanity

In Duo Survival, every decision counts. As you navigate through each level, your choices will shape the destiny of humanity. Will you and your partner successfully navigate the dangers and find the cure, or will you succumb to the relentless zombie horde?

Master the Controls

Stay sharp and coordinated with intuitive controls. Use AD or Left/Right arrow keys to move, W or Up arrow key to jump, and S or Down arrow key to kick or throw bottles. With precise control and teamwork, you can outsmart the zombies and emerge victorious.
