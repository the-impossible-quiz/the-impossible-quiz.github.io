Description

Embark on an Epic Viking Adventure in Duo Vikings

Prepare for a thrilling cooperative journey in Duo Vikings, a captivating 2-player game crafted by 7Spot Games. Join forces with two courageous Vikings as they embark on a daring raid through a majestic castle fraught with obstacles and challenges.

Conquer the Castle and Claim its Riches

Step into the shoes of these intrepid warriors and explore the castle's ornate halls teeming with riches and treasures waiting to be seized. Work together with your friend to navigate through perilous levels, solving puzzles, and overcoming obstacles along the way.

Master Exciting Puzzles and Challenges

Collaborate closely with your partner to trigger mechanisms, unlock doors, activate elevators, and shatter breakable objects. Test your coordination and problem-solving skills as you strategize your way through each level of this captivating castle.

Controls

Coordinate your movements with your teammate using intuitive controls:

  • Move: A/D or Left/Right arrow keys
  • Jump: W or Up arrow key
  • Hit/Use: S or Down arrow key

Join forces, overcome challenges, and carve your path to glory in Duo Vikings as you earn your place in the legendary halls of Valhalla!
