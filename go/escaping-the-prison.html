Description

Embark on a Daring Escape in Escaping the Prison

Join Henry Stickmin in his thrilling quest to break free from prison in Escaping the Prison, the second chapter of the Henry Stickmin legacy series. Your mission is clear: evade the vigilant guards and make a daring escape. With a range of tools at your disposal, including a file, cellphone, drill, and even a rocket launcher, your fate rests in your hands. Choose wisely, as each decision could lead to a different outcome!

Discover Multiple Endings

Will you choose the Lame Way, the Sneaky Way, or the Badass Way to freedom? Explore all the possible paths and endings as you navigate through the challenges of prison life. With multiple endings to uncover, the adventure never ends!

How to Play Escaping the Prison

Interact with objects using the left mouse button. Utilize the various tools available at each level to outsmart the guards and progress through the game. Dive into the immersive world of Henry Stickmin and plot your escape strategy in this exciting browser-based adventure!
