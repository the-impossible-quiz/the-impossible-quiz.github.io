Welcome to Flappy Bird!

Prepare for a challenging and addictive gaming experience with Flappy Bird, a classic title playable directly in your web browser. Test your reflexes and determination as you guide a tiny bird through a series of obstacles, aiming for the highest score possible.

Master the Basic Mechanics

Get acquainted with the fundamental gameplay mechanics of Flappy Bird. Keep the bird airborne by tapping your screen, steering clear of both the ground and the menacing pipes. Navigate the bird through the narrow gaps between pipes to accumulate points and progress further.

Strive for High Scores

Set your sights on achieving impressive high scores in Flappy Bird. Challenge yourself to surpass your previous best by skillfully maneuvering through as many pipes as possible. With each successful passage, your score increases, offering a sense of accomplishment and satisfaction.

Tips and Tricks for Success

Enhance your performance in Flappy Bird with these valuable tips and tricks. Develop a consistent tapping rhythm to maintain steady flight and avoid erratic movements. Stay focused on the upcoming obstacles and exercise patience to react promptly to the challenges ahead. Utilize short, controlled taps to keep the bird's flight stable, preventing sudden crashes and maximizing your chances of success.
