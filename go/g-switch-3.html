Introduction to G-Switch 3

G-Switch 3 is the latest installment in the adrenaline-pumping series that challenges your reflexes and coordination. Prepare to defy gravity and navigate through treacherous courses filled with obstacles, traps, and gravity switches.

Experience Gravity-Defying Action

Embark on an exhilarating journey where you control the laws of gravity. With each press of a button, you'll flip gravity, allowing your character to run on the ceiling, walls, and even upside-down. Master the art of timing and precision to overcome the game's increasingly challenging levels.

Test Your Reflexes

G-Switch 3 puts your reflexes to the ultimate test as you navigate through dynamic environments filled with obstacles. Stay sharp and react quickly to switch gravity at the right moment to avoid falling or getting stuck.

Compete with Friends

Challenge your friends in multiplayer mode and see who can survive the longest in this gravity-defying race. With synchronized gameplay, you'll experience heart-pounding moments as you compete for the top spot on the leaderboard.

How to Play

Playing G-Switch 3 is simple:

  • Switch Gravity: Press the left mouse button or any keyboard key to flip gravity and change your character's orientation.

Get ready to defy gravity and embark on an epic adventure in G-Switch 3!
