Experience Gravity-Defying Action with G-Switch 4

G-Switch 4, the latest installment in the G-Switch series, takes gravity-controlling gameplay to new heights. Prepare to defy gravity and test your skills in this thrilling platformer.

Play with Friends or Go Solo

In G-Switch 4, you have the option to play solo and explore the game's world to uncover its secrets. Alternatively, challenge up to 7 friends in the exhilarating multiplayer mode for an even more intense experience.

Create and Share Your Own Levels

One of the most exciting features of G-Switch 4 is the ability to design your own levels. Unleash your creativity and craft challenging stages that will push players to their limits. Share your creations with the global community and see how they fare!

How to Play G-Switch 4

To control the gravity-defying action, simply use the left mouse button or any keyboard key to switch gravity and navigate through the dynamic levels.
