Experience High-Speed Racing Action in Go Kart Go! Ultra!

Get ready for thrilling kart racing excitement in Go Kart Go! Ultra! Choose your driver from a colorful cast of characters and race through exhilarating tracks such as Old Station, Central Park, Watermill Mine, and more. Play Go Kart Go! Ultra! on Poki and collect stars to unlock new characters, each with their own unique abilities and traits.

Compete Against Friends in Two-Player Racing Mode

Challenge your friends to a head-to-head competition in the two-player racing mode. Use your driving skills and cunning strategy to outmaneuver opponents and claim victory on the track. Win a round of Go Kart Go! Ultra online and earn ultimate bragging rights!

Master the Controls for Ultimate Performance

With intuitive controls, including arrow keys or WASD for steering, space or Q for using items, shift or tab for jumping and drifting, and Z or E for looking back, you'll have everything you need to dominate the racetrack. Press Esc/P to pause the game and M to mute sound, ensuring you stay focused on the intense action!
