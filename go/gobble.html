Introduction to Gobble

Embark on a quirky arcade adventure in Gobble, where you take on the role of a hungry mouth residing in the ground. Your objective? Devour everything in sight, except for the people! Navigate through various puzzles as you gobble up everything from cacti and rocks to trees and boxes. Each level presents new challenges and tricks to keep you on your toes, ensuring an engaging gameplay experience from start to finish. How far can you go without consuming any people?

Gameplay Overview

In Gobble, you'll explore different sections of the ground, encountering puzzles and obstacles along the way. Strategically maneuver your mouth to consume objects while avoiding people, and uncover the solutions to each unique challenge.

Challenge Your Appetite

With each new section offering fresh challenges and surprises, Gobble promises hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Test your skills, solve puzzles, and see if you can satisfy your hunger without breaking the rules!

How to Play

Get started in Gobble with these controls:

  • Movement: Use the arrow keys, WASD, or click and drag with the mouse to navigate the ground and devour objects.

Prepare for a delightful feast of puzzles and challenges as you delve into the world of Gobble!
