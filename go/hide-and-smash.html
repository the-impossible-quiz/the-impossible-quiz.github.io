Description

Experience the Thrills of Hide and Smash

Dive into the exhilarating world of Hide and Smash, an upgraded version of the classic hide and seek game! Whether you find yourself on the blue hide team or the red smash team, get ready for an adrenaline-fueled adventure filled with suspense and strategy.

Hide or Smash, the Choice is Yours

As a member of the hide team, your mission is to stealthily navigate the environment, disguising yourself as everyday objects while collecting keys to secure victory. Meanwhile, if you're part of the smash team, your objective is to uncover and smash your hidden opponents before they can complete their mission.

A Battle of Strength and Wits

Hide and Smash pits players against each other in a thrilling showdown where quick thinking and cunning strategies are essential. Will you outsmart your opponents as a hider, or use your keen observational skills to track them down as a smasher?

How to Play Hide and Smash

Master the controls with ease using WASD or the arrow keys to move around the game environment. When you're ready to unleash your smashing prowess, simply left-click to aim and smash your way to victory!
