Description

Enter the Battlefield of Hills of Steel

Prepare for intense tank warfare in Hills of Steel, an action-packed shooter game that pits you against enemy forces on hilly terrain. Take control of a powerful tank as you race and shoot your way through the battlefield, facing off against tanks, mechs, and robots.

Unlock and Upgrade Your Arsenal

With a variety of tanks, mechs, and robots to unlock, customize your arsenal to suit your combat style. Enhance your tank's capabilities with powerful Boosters such as mines, force shields, and rocket strikes, making it even more formidable on the battlefield.

Conquer the Hills of Steel

Do you have what it takes to build the ultimate tank and dominate the battlefield? Test your skills as you navigate through challenging terrain, engage in thrilling shootouts, and emerge victorious in the Hills of Steel!

How to Play

Drive your tank using the A and D keys or Arrow keys. Use the spacebar to shoot at enemies and the B button to repair your tank when needed. Strategize your movements and firepower to outmaneuver and outgun your opponents!
