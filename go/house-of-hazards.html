Introduction to House of Hazards

House of Hazards invites you to embark on a hilarious multiplayer adventure where chaos reigns supreme. Navigate through a series of perilous obstacles and challenges as you compete against your friends in a race to victory. Created with interactive mayhem in mind, this game promises endless laughter and entertainment for players of all ages.

Experience Wacky Multiplayer Fun

Prepare to face a variety of hazards and traps as you and your friends vie for supremacy in the House of Hazards. From dodging flying objects to navigating treacherous platforms, every moment is filled with unpredictable moments and uproarious laughter.

Compete Against Friends

Challenge your friends to a series of multiplayer showdowns as you battle your way through the hazardous house. With intuitive controls and fast-paced gameplay, House of Hazards offers an immersive gaming experience that keeps you on the edge of your seat.

How to Play

Playing House of Hazards is simple:

  • Player 1 Controls: Use A to move left, D to move right, W to jump, and S to crouch/fire.
  • Player 2 Controls: Use J to move left, L to move right, K to jump, and I to crouch/fire.

Prepare for laughter, excitement, and unpredictable challenges in House of Hazards!
