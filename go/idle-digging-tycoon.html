Introducing Idle Digging Tycoon

Embark on a journey to a distant world inhabited by cavemen in Idle Digging Tycoon, a casual idle game developed by No Power-up Studios. Take on the role of a tycoon and hire cavemen to dig the ground in search of precious gold and valuable treasures.

Build Your Empire

Utilize the gold you've earned from your excavations to construct new buildings, unlock upgrades, and acquire faster tools. Expand your empire and turn this shapeless map into a thriving business venture for you and your team of cavemen.

Join the Digging Craze

How to Play Idle Digging Tycoon

Tap with your finger or click with your left mouse button repeatedly to dig the ground and unearth valuable resources. Manage your resources wisely and strategically invest in upgrades to maximize your profits.
