Introducing Idle Lumber Inc.

Step into the world of timber management with Idle Lumber Inc., a captivating simulation game developed by NoPowerup. Build and expand your own lumber empire as you strive to become the ultimate lumber tycoon!

Grow Your Empire

Begin your journey by establishing a small sawmill and hiring a team of workers. As you progress, invest in upgrading your tools, employees, and managers to expand your factory and increase efficiency. Purchase additional lands to grow vast forests and ensure sustainable harvesting practices.

Optimize Operations

Train your lumberjacks to enhance their skills, enabling them to harvest logs more efficiently. Implement strategic marketing campaigns to attract new clients and process orders with precision to maximize profits. With careful management, your lumber empire will flourish.

Become the Top Lumber Tycoon

Take on the challenge of building the biggest lumber empire in Idle Lumber Inc. Explore a variety of gameplay options and strategic decisions as you climb the ranks to claim the title of the number one lumber tycoon.

How to Play Idle Lumber Inc.

Interact with various elements in the game by tapping or clicking with your left mouse button. Take control of your lumber operations and make strategic decisions to optimize production and maximize profits.
