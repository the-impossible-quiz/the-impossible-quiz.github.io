Description

Engage in Epic Pig vs. Wolf Battles in Iron Snout

Prepare for an adrenaline-pumping online fighting experience in Iron Snout, a captivating game crafted by SnoutUp. Assume the role of a brave pig determined to fend off hordes of menacing wolves and other adversaries. Utilize your martial arts skills to punch, kick, and flip your way through relentless waves of enemies.

Show the Wolves Who's Boss

Take a stand against the ferocious wolves and demonstrate your prowess in combat. Dodge incoming axes and projectiles with lightning-fast reflexes using the arrow keys. Iron Snout challenges you to prove that pigs are not to be trifled with.

Experience Thrilling Multiplayer Modes

Team up with a friend and dive into the action-packed 2-player Wolfieball mode, where you and your partner can face off against the relentless wolf onslaught together. Alternatively, tackle the wolves solo in Classic and Sudden Death modes, showcasing your skills in solo combat.

Unleash the Festive Spirit with the Christmas Update

This year, Iron Snout receives a festive makeover with a delightful Santa-snoutfit. Additionally, seize the opportunity to win exciting prizes by completing various in-game achievements, adding an extra layer of excitement to your gameplay experience.

How to Play

Master the art of combat using simple controls:

  • Use arrow keys for jumping and striking wolves.

Embark on an epic journey, defy the odds, and emerge victorious as you take on the wolves in Iron Snout!
