Description

Step up to the Crease in Little Master Cricket

Little Master Cricket is an engaging sports game that puts your batting skills to the test. With its ragdoll physics engine and unique control scheme, you'll feel like you're right in the middle of the cricket action.

Master the Bat and Score Big

With direct control of the bat, you have the power to play defensively and build your innings or go for big shots to score quick runs. Swing the bat to hit the ball into the striped scoring zones, but be careful to avoid sending it to the yellow-and-black zones, where you risk being caught out!

Compete for the Title of Little Master

Only those with sharp concentration and precise timing will earn the coveted title of Little Master and be declared world champion. Share the game with your friends to challenge them and multiply the fun!

How to Play Little Master Cricket

Simply swing the bat to hit the ball into the striped scoring zones while avoiding the yellow-and-black zones. Keep your wits about you and defend your stumps to stay in the game and rack up the runs!
