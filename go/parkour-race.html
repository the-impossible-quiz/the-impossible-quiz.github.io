Introduction to Parkour Race

Experience the adrenaline-fueled thrill of 3D parkour racing in Parkour Race, a dynamic running game developed by Madbox. Strap on your running shoes, take control of your stickman, and dash through the urban landscape as you compete against the crowd in a high-stakes race.

Gameplay Overview

In Parkour Race, you'll navigate through a sprawling cityscape, leaping from rooftop to rooftop with precision and style. Run, dash, and jump over obstacles as you strive to outpace your competitors and reach the finish line in record time.

Master the Moves

With a variety of maneuvers at your disposal, including vaults, flips, and dashes, you can showcase your parkour skills and leave the competition in awe. Collect glowing speed bumpers to gain momentum and propel yourself to victory!

How to Play

Get started in Parkour Race with these simple controls:

  • Move Left: Use the A key or the Left arrow key to veer left through the urban maze.
  • Move Right: Utilize the D key or the Right arrow key to navigate right, avoiding obstacles and racing towards the finish line.

Race to Glory

With daily challenges, power-ups, and customizable accessories, Parkour Race offers endless excitement and opportunities for competitive fun. So lace up your shoes, hit the pavement, and take your running game to the next level with Parkour Race!
