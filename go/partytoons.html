Introducing Idle PartyToons Inc.

Embark on a whimsical journey of mini-game mayhem with Idle PartyToons Inc., a delightful party game developed by TinyDobbins. Gather your friends or team up with strangers in exhilarating four-player matches packed with fun-filled challenges!

Mini-Game Madness

Prepare for a diverse array of mini-games, each with its own unique rules and gameplay styles. From dodging falling rocks to navigating obstacle-filled conveyor belts, popping risky balloons, and unraveling mysterious boxes, there's no shortage of excitement in PartyToons!

Competitive Fun

Outsmart your opponents and showcase your quick reflexes as you compete to secure the most points in each mini-game. Whether it's acting faster or making wiser decisions, every move counts in the quest for victory.

Unlockable Characters

Utilize your hard-earned points and trophies to unlock new playable characters and customize your gaming experience. With a variety of characters to choose from, you can tailor your roster to suit your playstyle and preferences.

Share the Joy

Spread the joy of Idle PartyToons by inviting your friends to join the fun on Poki. With endless opportunities for laughter and excitement, Idle PartyToons Inc. is the perfect addition to any gathering or afternoon hangout!

Controls:

Player 1: Arrow keys
Player 2: WASD
Player 3: 8456
Player 4: UHJK
