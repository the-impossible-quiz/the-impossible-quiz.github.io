Protect Your Treasure in Raft Wars

Embark on an epic adventure in Raft Wars, a thrilling level-based shooting game developed by Martijn Kunst. Join forces with your brother Simon as you defend your precious treasure from a host of adversaries! Armed with just a raft and some tennis balls at first, hone your aim and power to defeat formidable foes ranging from vikings to pirates and more. Knock your opponents off their rafts or deplete their health to emerge victorious!

Upgrade Your Arsenal

As you progress, strategically earn coins by landing precise shots to unlock powerful weapons like grenades, rockets, and upgrades for your raft. With each new level presenting unique challenges, adapt your strategy and firepower to overcome increasingly difficult opponents and protect your treasure at all costs!

How to Play Raft Wars

Take aim and shoot by clicking and dragging with your mouse. With intuitive controls and addictive gameplay, Raft Wars offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages. Play now and test your shooting skills in this thrilling adventure!
