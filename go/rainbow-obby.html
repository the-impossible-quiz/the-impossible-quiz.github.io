Introduction to Rainbow Obby

Welcome to Rainbow Obby, the ultimate obstacle course adventure that will test your agility and determination! Embark on a thrilling journey as you strive to reach the pinnacle of a colossal obstacle course, with three unique game modes to choose from.

Game Modes

  • Car Mode: For the speed demons and skilled drivers, this mode is packed with ramps, jumps, and tricky roads. Race against the clock as you tackle challenging courses designed to put your driving skills to the test!
