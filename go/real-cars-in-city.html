Description

Experience the Thrill of Real Cars in City

Real Cars in City offers an immersive 3D simulation experience where you can showcase your driving prowess across a variety of unique race tracks. Challenge AI opponents or compete against friends, collecting stars as you successfully complete races to unlock a diverse range of cool new cars.

Customize and Upgrade Your Ride

Utilize the stars and money earned to customize and enhance your vehicle to match your preferred playstyle. With numerous impressive vehicles to choose from and various customization options available, you can create your ultimate driving machine.

Explore Exciting Features

Real Cars in City features a two-player mode, a free drive area, and a thrilling battlefield area complete with Hot Pursuit action. Don't forget to complete side quests to earn rewards and save up for your dream car!

How to Play

Player 1 controls: Drive with WASD or Arrow keys, activate Nitro with N, and change camera view with C. Player 2 controls: Use WASD or Arrow keys to drive, and activate Nitro with T.
