Description

Experience High-Octane Soccer Action with Rocket Soccer Derby!

Rocket Soccer Derby offers an electrifying fusion of soccer, car racing, and simulation gameplay all rolled into one exhilarating package. Take control of your car as you navigate the football pitch, aiming to outscore your opponent in a thrilling match of rocket-powered soccer. Whether you opt for a quick game or dive into the competitive Rocket League, there's never a dull moment on the field!

Compete in the Rocket League for Glory and Rewards

Join the Rocket League and vie for victory against skilled opponents to earn in-game currency and cogs, which you can use to upgrade your car. Start your journey in the Beginners League, hone your skills, and progress through the ranks to the Intermediate League and beyond. With dedication and skill, you might even ascend to the prestigious Expert League!

Master the Controls and Dominate the Pitch

Get ready to command the field with precision using the following controls:

  • Drive: WASD
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Change Camera View: F

Get behind the wheel, unleash your skills, and lead your team to victory in Rocket Soccer Derby!
