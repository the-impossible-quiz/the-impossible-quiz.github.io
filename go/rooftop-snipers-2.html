Introducing Rooftop Snipers 2

Experience adrenaline-pumping rooftop battles in Rooftop Snipers 2, an exhilarating online shooting game crafted by New Eich Games and released in 2019. Test your sniper prowess as you engage in heart-pounding duels atop towering buildings, where every shot counts in determining your survival.

Master the Rooftops

Your objective in Rooftop Snipers 2 is simple: showcase your sniper skills to outmaneuver your opponent and maintain your foothold on the roof. Engage in thrilling showdowns with a friend or rival player, each vying for dominance in this intense rooftop arena.

Customize Your Experience

Unleash your creativity by customizing the playing arena to your preferences, ensuring every matchup is uniquely tailored to your liking. Unlock a diverse array of characters, each armed and ready for battle, as you strive to emerge victorious in epic rooftop skirmishes.

How to Play Rooftop Snipers 2?

Jump: W and I keys

Shoot: E and O keys

Are you ready to take aim, leap into action, and prove yourself as the ultimate rooftop sniper? Dive into Rooftop Snipers 2 and reign supreme atop the city skyline!
