Introducing Rooftop Snipers

Rooftop Snipers is an addictive two-player shooting game set atop towering buildings. Step into the shoes of a skilled sniper and engage in thrilling sniper duels against your opponent. With its simple yet engaging gameplay mechanics, Rooftop Snipers promises hours of competitive fun.

Engage in Sniper Duels

Challenge your friends or take on the AI in fast-paced sniper battles. Aim carefully, time your shots, and outsmart your opponent to emerge victorious. Whether you prefer facing off against a friend or testing your skills against the computer, Rooftop Snipers offers intense sniper action for players of all levels.

Master the Rooftop Arena

Use the environment to your advantage as you navigate the rooftop arena. Dodge incoming bullets, utilize cover, and strategically position yourself to gain the upper hand. With its dynamic gameplay and unpredictable encounters, Rooftop Snipers keeps you on the edge of your seat.

Are you ready to take aim and dominate the rooftop battlefield in Rooftop Snipers?
