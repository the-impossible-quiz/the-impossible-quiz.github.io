Description

Welcome to Shady Bears!

Embark on an adorable adventure in Shady Bears, a thrilling platform game where you guide a charming bear on a quest to collect delicious acorns. But beware, your shadow is your greatest foe, mimicking your every move and chasing you relentlessly!

Beware of Shadows and Bees

Avoid your own shadow as it trails behind you, ready to pounce at any moment. Collect honey to boost your acorn count, but watch out for the bees that are attracted to the sweet nectar.

Team Up with Friends

Join forces with friends in multiplayer mode and work together to outsmart your shadows. Coordinate your moves and gather acorns collaboratively to achieve victory!

Utilize Helpful Fireflies

Keep an eye out for friendly fireflies that can help you by eliminating shadows, giving you a temporary advantage in your quest for acorns.

How to Play Shady Bears

For single-player mode, use the Left and Right arrow keys to move, and press the Space bar to jump. In two-player mode, one player controls movement and jumping with the Up/Left/Right arrow keys, while the other player uses AWD/QZD for movement and jumping.
