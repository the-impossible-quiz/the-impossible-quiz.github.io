Description

Welcome to Shape Fold!

Dive into the captivating world of Shape Fold, a mesmerizing puzzle game crafted by Bikas. Unlike traditional jigsaw puzzles, Shape Fold offers a refreshing twist by introducing interconnected puzzle pieces that interact with each other, providing a unique challenge for puzzle enthusiasts.

Challenge Your Mind

Your objective in Shape Fold is to manipulate individual puzzle pieces and arrange them in such a way that they seamlessly form the underlying object. With each piece intricately connected, you'll need to employ strategic thinking and spatial reasoning to conquer each puzzle.

Explore Diverse Themes

Immerse yourself in a rich tapestry of themes as you traverse through aesthetically pleasing levels featuring objects inspired by history, culture, nature, animals, and more. Each puzzle presents a new and exciting folding experience!

How to Play Shape Fold

Simply use your mouse cursor or finger to drag and manipulate shapes and pieces across the screen. Piece together the puzzle with precision and finesse to unlock the full potential of Shape Fold!
