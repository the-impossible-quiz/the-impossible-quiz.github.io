Description

Embark on a Perilous Journey in Short Life

Short Life plunges you into a world where danger lurks around every corner. Navigate through treacherous obstacles, including swinging blades, explosive mines, and deadly traps, as you strive to survive against all odds. Test your mettle and see how long you can endure in this heart-pounding adventure. Best of all, you can play Short Life for free, so dive in and embrace the challenge!

Choose Your Character and Conquer the Challenges

Select from a variety of playable characters and unlock more by collecting stars scattered throughout the levels. Each character brings its own unique style to the game, adding depth and variety to your gameplay experience. Explore the online world of Short Life and immerse yourself in a crash course of death-defying obstacles!

Master the Controls and Navigate the Danger

Take control of your character with precision using the following controls:

  • Left and Right Arrow Keys: Move left and right
  • Up Arrow: Jump / Stand up (if crouching)
  • Down Arrow: Crouch

Prepare for a thrilling adventure where every move counts in Short Life. Are you ready to face the ultimate test of survival?
