Description

Defend the City in Skibidi Shooter

Get ready for an intense shooting experience in Skibidi Shooter, where you must fend off the invading Skibidi Toilets that threaten the city! Armed with a handgun, sniper rifle, and machine gun, you'll battle your way through various levels to protect the city from the relentless onslaught.

Join the Cameramen Resistance

As a skilled agent of the Cameramen resistance, it's up to you to stand against the Skibidi Toilets and push them back from the city streets. Whether you prefer precision shooting or rapid-fire action, there's a weapon suited to your playstyle as you fight to defend the city.

Face Endless Waves of Enemies

For the most daring agents, Endless Mode offers a relentless challenge where you can test your skills against endless waves of Skibidi Toilets. How long can you survive against the relentless onslaught?

How to Play Skibidi Shooter

To take down the invading Skibidi Toilets, simply use your mouse to aim and shoot. As the battle intensifies, make use of the 1, 2, and 3 keys to switch between your different weapons and adapt to the changing threats.

Join the resistance, defend the city, and prove your shooting prowess in Skibidi Shooter!
