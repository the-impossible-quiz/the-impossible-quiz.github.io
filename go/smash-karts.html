Introducing Smash Karts

Smash Karts is an adrenaline-fueled multiplayer racing game that combines high-speed kart racing with chaotic combat. Get ready to race against friends or AI opponents in action-packed battles across colorful tracks filled with obstacles and power-ups.

Race and Battle

Take control of your kart and navigate through challenging courses while fending off opponents with an array of weapons. Blast your rivals with rockets, drop banana peels to create hazards, and unleash chaos to secure victory on the track.

Master the Controls

With intuitive controls, you can easily maneuver your kart around tight corners, dodge incoming attacks, and unleash devastating firepower. Whether you're using the WASD keys or arrow keys for movement, and the spacebar to fire weapons, you'll experience fast-paced action and intense competition in Smash Karts.

Are you ready to race, battle, and dominate the track in Smash Karts?
