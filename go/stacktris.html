Description

Experience the Thrill of Stacktris!

Get ready for an addictive arcade experience with Stacktris, a game that combines the classic Tetris gameplay with the challenge of tower stacking! Test your balancing skills as you stack and balance tetromino blocks without letting them fall. Stacktris offers a unique twist on the Tetris formula, providing hours of entertainment and excitement.

Stack and Balance Tetrominoes

In Stacktris, your objective is simple yet challenging: stack tetromino blocks on top of each other without letting them topple over. As the game progresses, the blocks spin faster, putting your reflexes and precision to the test. Tap to stop the spin of the tetromino, drag to position it above the platform, and release to drop. Carefully plan your moves to build the tallest tower possible!

Unlock Power-Ups and Upgrades

As you play Stacktris, you'll collect coins that can be used to unlock helpful power-ups and upgrades. From Slow Spin and High Friction to Coin Magnet and Wide Table, these enhancements will make your stacking experience easier and more enjoyable. Experiment with different strategies and power-ups to reach new heights in Stacktris!

Challenge Your Friends and Aim for the Sky!

Stacktris offers endless fun and competition, perfect for challenging your friends and proving your stacking skills. Share your high scores and tallest Tetromino Tower with your friends, and see who can stack the highest! With its simple controls and addictive gameplay, Stacktris is a must-play for fans of Tetris and tower stacking games alike.

How to Play Stacktris?

Desktop: Click and hold to stop the tetrominos from spinning, drag your mouse to move them horizontally, and release to drop. Use the A/D keys or Left/Right arrow keys to move them horizontally. Stack the blocks as high as you can without knocking them down!

Mobile/Tablet: Long-press to stop the tetrominos from spinning, drag horizontally to position them, and release to drop. Stack the blocks carefully to build the tallest tower and beat your own records!
