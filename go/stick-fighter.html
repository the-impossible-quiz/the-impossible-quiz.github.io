Description

Welcome to Stick Fighter!

Step into the arena and become a master of combat in Stick Fighter, a thrilling fighting game where you control one of six unique characters, each with their own special moves and abilities. Whether you prefer to go solo in single-player mode or challenge a friend to a head-to-head battle, Stick Fighter offers endless excitement and fierce competition!

Choose Your Champion

With six characters to choose from, each with their own distinct fighting style, the choice is yours. Will you specialize in mastering the techniques of one character, or will you rise to the challenge and become proficient in them all? The path to victory is in your hands!

Master the Moves

Utilize a variety of attacks and maneuvers to outwit and outmaneuver your opponents. From basic strikes to advanced combos, hone your skills and unleash devastating attacks to claim victory in every bout.

How to Play Stick Fighter

Control your character's movement with the A/D keys or the left/right arrow keys. Use the same keys to execute basic attacks. Experiment with different combinations of keys to perform special moves, double jumps, and powerful aerial attacks. Are you ready to prove yourself as the ultimate Stick Fighter?
