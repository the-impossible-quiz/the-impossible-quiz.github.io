Introduction to Stick Merge

Enter the world of Stick Merge, a casual action and merging game crafted by TinyDobbins. Prepare to embark on an exciting journey where you merge various weapons to create powerful guns, then unleash them upon moving stick figures in the shooting range. With a focus on strategy and precision, Stick Merge offers thrilling gameplay that will keep you engaged for hours.

Gameplay Overview

In Stick Merge, your objective is to combine different types of weapons to unlock stronger firearms and take down your targets with precision. Upgrade your pistol, experiment with power-ups, and hone your shooting skills to become the ultimate marksman in town.

Master the Mechanics

As you progress in Stick Merge, you'll discover new ways to merge weapons and optimize your arsenal for maximum firepower. Strategize your moves, select the right weapons, and aim carefully to achieve the highest scores.

How to Play

Get started in Stick Merge with these simple instructions:

  • Select and Move Weapons: Click and drag the left mouse button to select and move weapons.
  • Shoot Enemies: Drag and move the mouse cursor to aim and shoot at moving stick figures.

Prepare for action-packed merging and shooting fun in Stick Merge as you strive to become the best shooter in town!
