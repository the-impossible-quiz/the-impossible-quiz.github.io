Introduction to Stickman Climb 2

Embark on a thrilling platforming adventure in Stickman Climb 2, where you'll guide a stickman through treacherous terrain using only a pickaxe to maintain balance. Your goal? Reach the flag at the end of each level while navigating past dangerous traps and spikes. With checkpoints scattered along the way, precision and caution are key to your success.

Gameplay Overview

In Stickman Climb 2, you'll test your agility and reflexes as you maneuver through increasingly challenging levels. Customize your stickman avatar with a variety of colors, vessels, and weapons as you progress, adding a personal touch to your climbing experience.

Challenge Your Friends

Whether you're tackling the adventure solo or competing against a friend, Stickman Climb 2 offers excitement for players of all skill levels. Race against your friend locally and see who can conquer the climbing challenges first!

How to Play

Get started in Stickman Climb 2 with these simple controls:

  • Player 1: Use A / D keys to move left and right, and press R to respawn.
  • Player 2: Utilize the Left / Right arrow keys to navigate, and press Enter to respawn.

Master the art of balance, conquer each level, and emerge victorious in Stickman Climb 2!
