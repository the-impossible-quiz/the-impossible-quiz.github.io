Description

Experience Thrilling Stunts in Stunt Car Challenge 3

Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled adventure in Stunt Car Challenge 3! This action-packed stunt car game puts you behind the wheel of powerful vehicles as you race across challenging tracks, performing jaw-dropping stunts along the way.

Conquer the Tracks and Collect Coins

Race against the clock to achieve fast times while executing incredible stunts. Perform front and back flips to gather coins scattered throughout the tracks. Aim for a perfect 3-star rating by collecting all the coins and showcasing your stunt-driving skills.

Unlock New Areas and Vehicles

With each star you earn, unlock the thrilling Port area featuring a host of new levels to conquer. Choose from 15 awesome vehicles, each offering unique abilities and performance. Unlock upgrades such as engine enhancements, tire improvements, and suspension modifications to optimize your car's performance.

Customize and Dominate

Personalize your vehicle with a variety of paint jobs to make it stand out on the track. Upgrade your car to its fullest potential and dominate the competition as you master each level and earn those coveted 3 stars.

How to Play

Control your car's throttle with the up arrow and tilt it forward or backward using the left and right arrow keys. Navigate through challenging obstacles, execute flawless stunts, and race your way to become the ultimate Stunt Car Challenge driver!
