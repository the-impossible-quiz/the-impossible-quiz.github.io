Description

Experience Floppy Football Fun in Super Liquid Soccer

Super Liquid Soccer, created by Punyrobot, offers a unique twist on traditional soccer gameplay. Choose your favorite country and dive into the world of floppy football! With a variety of playing styles, including chip passes and through balls, discover your own strategy and aim to become the ultimate World Champion!

Master the Art of Penalties

Test your skills in nail-biting penalty shootouts! Lace up your boots, step up to the spot, and prepare to both take and save penalties. Can you handle the pressure and lead your team to victory?

How to Play Super Liquid Soccer

Control your player's movement using the WASD keys. Pass the ball with M, shoot with J, perform chip passes with L, and execute through passes with I. Switch between players using Q or E, and sprint using P or O. With these controls at your fingertips, dominate the field and claim soccer glory!
