Description

Experience Thrilling Racing Action in Super Star Car

Super Star Car invites you to dive into the world of Formula-1 racing with its exhilarating 3D gameplay. Created by Barnzmu, this racing game offers a collection of vehicles and circuits to kickstart your racing career.

Immerse Yourself in Stunning 3D Graphics

With stunning 3D graphics and smooth vehicle controls, Super Star Car delivers a realistic racing experience that will keep you on the edge of your seat as you race at full speed around dynamic tracks.

Customize and Upgrade Your Vehicle

As you earn money through racing, invest in performance boosts and mechanical improvements to enhance your vehicle's capabilities. Feel the thrill of speeding past your opponents with every upgrade you make!

How to Play Super Star Car

To navigate your way through the intense races, use the WASD or arrow keys to drive your car. If you find yourself in a tight spot, use the space bar to respawn. Additionally, change your perspective with the C key to enjoy different camera views and immerse yourself fully in the action.
