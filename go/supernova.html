Description

Embark on an Interstellar Adventure in Supernova

Supernova invites you to embark on an exhilarating journey through the depths of space, where your piloting skills will be put to the ultimate test. Maneuver your ship through a dazzling array of obstacles as you navigate through visually stunning and procedurally generated stages.

Experience Intense Challenges

As you progress through the game, your speed will steadily increase, presenting you with increasingly complex challenges to overcome. With each passing obstacle, your adrenaline will surge as you strive to beat your own streak and set new records.

Unlock Multiple Vehicles

Supernova offers a variety of spacecraft for you to unlock, each with its own unique characteristics and attributes. Experiment with different vehicles and find the one that best suits your piloting style.

Controls

Take control of your spacecraft with ease using the following controls:

  • Use the WASD or arrow keys to change the ship's direction.

Prepare for an out-of-this-world experience as you navigate the treacherous obstacles of Supernova!
