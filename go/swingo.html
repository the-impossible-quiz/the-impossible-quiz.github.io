Description

Embark on an Adventure with Swingo!

Step into the world of Swingo, an exciting arcade game that blends platforming and action elements into an engaging experience. Take control of a charming bouncy character equipped with a grappling hook, your key to navigating through the game's dynamic levels. Fire the hook and swing your way through obstacles to reach the coveted fruit at the end of each stage!

Unlock Cool Characters and Discover Hidden Surprises

As you progress through Swingo, you'll earn points for completing levels successfully. Accumulate enough points to unlock a variety of adorable characters, starting with a delightful frog. Can you unlock them all and master each one's unique abilities? Explore vibrant and creatively designed levels filled with surprises waiting to be discovered!

How to Play Swingo

Use your mouse to aim and shoot the grappling hook. Click, drag, and release to launch the hook and propel your character in the desired direction. With precise timing and skillful maneuvering, you'll swing your way to victory in Swingo!
