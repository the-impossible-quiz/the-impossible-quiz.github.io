Introduction to Tag

Get ready for an exciting game of catch in Tag, a local multiplayer experience that pits you against your friends in a thrilling chase! With up to four players, you'll race around three different levels, utilizing shortcuts and sneaky tactics to outmaneuver your opponents. Will you emerge victorious as the ultimate tag champion?

Gameplay Overview

In Tag, players square off against each other in a game of cat and mouse. Utilize the environment to your advantage, using shortcuts and teleportation to evade your pursuers and secure victory. With multiple levels to explore and endless strategies to employ, Tag promises endless fun and excitement for players of all ages.

Outrun Your Opponents

Challenge your friends in fast-paced multiplayer action and see who has what it takes to outrun the competition. With intuitive controls and dynamic gameplay, Tag offers an immersive experience that will keep you coming back for more.

How to Play

Get started in Tag with these movement controls:

  • Player 1: Use W, A, and D keys.
  • Player 2: Use Up, Right, and Left arrow keys.
  • Player 3: Use I, J, and L keys.
  • Player 4: Use T, F, and H keys.

To tag another character, simply walk into them. The player who is tagged will have a white arrow above them!

Get ready to run, dodge, and tag your way to victory in Tag!
