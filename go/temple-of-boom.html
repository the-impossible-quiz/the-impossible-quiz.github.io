Introduction to Temple of Boom

Prepare yourself for an explosive adventure in the Temple of Boom! This action-packed platform game plunges you into the heart of a treacherous temple filled with monstrous foes and deadly traps. Your survival depends on your ability to navigate through waves of relentless enemies and emerge victorious!

Embark on a Thrilling Campaign

Delve deep into the perilous depths of the temple in Campaign mode, where you'll confront a horde of otherworldly creatures across three ancient temples. Brace yourself for intense battles as you fight to escape the clutches of the temple's malevolent inhabitants.

Endless Challenges Await

In Endless mode, the adventure never ends. Your objective is simple: obliterate everything in your path and strive to stay alive against increasingly ferocious enemies. How long can you survive the relentless onslaught?

Controls

Mastering the controls is essential for your survival:

Single Player

  • Jump or Double Jump: Arrow Up
  • Move Sideways: Left/Right Arrow
  • Pick Up Weapon: Down Arrow
  • Shoot: Z
  • Switch Weapon: X

Two Player

Player 1:

  • Jump or Double Jump: W
  • Move Sideways: A/S
  • Pick Up Weapon: S
  • Shoot: C
  • Switch Weapon: V

Player 2:

  • Jump or Double Jump: Arrow Up
  • Move Sideways: Left/Right Arrow
  • Pick Up Weapon: Down Arrow
  • Shoot: K
  • Switch Weapon: L
