Introducing The Impossible Quiz

The Impossible Quiz is not your ordinary trivia game; it's a challenging test of wit and creativity. With over 110 mind-bending questions, each more perplexing than the last, this quiz will push your problem-solving skills to the limit.

Think Outside the Box

Prepare to encounter questions with double meanings, tricky wordplay, and unexpected twists. To succeed, you'll need to think outside the box and consider unconventional solutions. With only three lives at your disposal, every mistake could be costly.

Challenge Your Mind

This original game runs seamlessly on any device without the need for Flash. Test your intellect and see if you can conquer all 110 questions. And if you're up for even more brain-teasing fun, don't forget to try The Impossible Quiz 2.

How to Play

Click on the right answer from the four options provided. Utilize Skips to bypass particularly challenging questions, but beware of Bombs with countdown timers. Answer quickly or face the consequences.

Test Your Knowledge

For those curious about the first ten answers, here they are: Four, No, but a tin can, K.O, THE ANSWER, Move your mouse out of the game and around it, then back to "Next question", Shallots, An elephant, Find the green tick, Top right 'THAT ONE', and Dentures.

So, are you ready to take on the ultimate test of intellect? Dive into The Impossible Quiz and see if you have what it takes to emerge victorious!
