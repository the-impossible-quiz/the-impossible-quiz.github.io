Experience the Thrill of Top Speed 3D!

Get ready to unleash the ultimate racing experience in Top Speed 3D! Customize your dream car to perfection, from its color and wheels to its suspension and horsepower. If you encounter any mishaps, head to the auto shop for quick repairs and upgrades tailored to your specifications.

Customize Your Ride

Explore an empty city map and test drive your favorite sports cars without the worry of pedestrians. With the option to save your progress and return later, Top Speed 3D offers endless opportunities to hit top speeds and dominate the streets.

How to Play

Customize your car and hit the road, speeding through the city streets. Utilize the driving assistant if needed to get started, then race to victory and upgrade your car along the way!
