Description

Welcome to Village Craft!

Embark on a medieval adventure in Village Craft, an engaging idle/management game where you'll build and expand a bustling town from humble beginnings. Starting with just an axe in the wilderness, you'll transform the serene forest into a prosperous village!

Build and Expand

Begin by felling trees and constructing your first buildings to lay the foundation of your town. Watch as your settlement flourishes with each new structure you erect and each villager that joins your community.

Manage Resources and Villagers

Assign tasks to your villagers, who will work diligently to process resources like wood and wheat into valuable commodities. These items can be sold to travelers, providing you with the funds needed to further develop your village.

Grow Your Village

As your village thrives, attract more settlers and unlock new opportunities for expansion. Witness your once-sparse forest transform into a bustling hub of activity and commerce!

How to Play Village Craft

Move around the game world using the arrow keys, WASD, or by clicking and dragging with the mouse. Explore the wilderness, manage your resources, and build the village of your dreams!
