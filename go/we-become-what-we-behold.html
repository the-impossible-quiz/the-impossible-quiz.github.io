Introduction to We Become What We Behold

Welcome to We Become What We Behold, a thought-provoking browser game that explores the power of media and perception! Immerse yourself in a captivating experience where you'll witness the impact of your photography on society's behavior.

Gameplay Overview

In We Become What We Behold, you wield the camera as your tool to capture the essence of society. Drag the camera indicator around and click to take photographs of unique and eventful moments. But be mindful, as your snapshots will shape the behavior of those you observe.

Unravel Societal Dynamics

As you document various scenes, you'll notice how the subjects of your photographs react and interact with one another. Through your lens, you'll uncover the intricate web of societal dynamics and witness firsthand how perception influences behavior.

Reflect on Media Influence

We Become What We Behold challenges players to reflect on the power of media and its role in shaping our perceptions and actions. With each snapshot, you'll delve deeper into the complexities of human behavior and the far-reaching consequences of what we choose to behold.
