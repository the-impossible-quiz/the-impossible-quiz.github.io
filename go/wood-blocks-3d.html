Description

Experience Puzzle Fun with Wood Blocks 3D

Wood Blocks 3D, developed by Infinity Games, offers a refreshing take on the classic block puzzle genre. Unlike traditional Tetris games, you have the freedom to place the given blocks anywhere on the grid, adding a new layer of strategy to the gameplay.

Challenge Your Mind with Strategic Gameplay

In Wood Blocks 3D, you're tasked with filling entire rows or columns on the grid using the provided pieces. Drag and drop the blocks to clear lines and earn points. Utilize power-ups, create combos, and plan your moves carefully to achieve the highest score possible.

Relax and Train Your Brain

With its engaging gameplay and intuitive mechanics, Wood Blocks 3D provides the perfect balance of relaxation and mental stimulation. Whether you're unwinding after a long day or looking to sharpen your cognitive skills, this game offers hours of enjoyable puzzle-solving.

How to Play Wood Blocks 3D

Simply drag and drop the pieces onto the grid to fill entire rows or columns. Clearing lines earns you points. Additionally, use power-ups to remove or rotate pieces, adding another layer of strategy to your gameplay.
